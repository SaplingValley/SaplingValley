Inspiration.

I think Vanilla Ice said it best “If there was a problem, yo, I’ll solve it”. Although we cannot express with such lyrical wizardry, the words do strike at the core of what we have set out to achieve for all who have ever raised a JIRA issue.

The 3 of us have worked together for over 10 years. We manage different teams across development and support which are interconnected and dependent on each other to get our work done.

JIRA has always been there with us, and plays an integral part. But there was always something missing.

Elevator for JIRA was born because we had the same challenge you do. Knowing who you should assign an issue to. Getting issues to the right people in your business the first time and ensuring everyone is on the same page.

For us it was the support on-call person, the on-call development 3rd level support or the Project Manager on a project and so on.

We knew getting issues to the right people and alerting them that the issue was there would increase efficiency, get issues resolved faster and make us all look good.

Our days, like yours are busy enough, we have no time to chase issues and drive accountability for the people responsible to action them.

Go on, install a trial and give it a try. It may just change your life.
